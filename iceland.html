Iceland a volcanically and geologically active land between the North Atlantic and Arctic oceans, boasts beautiful landscapes characterised by sand, lava fields, mountains and glaciers. It was icy and misty when I was passing through a singularly dreary tract of Iceland in February 2013. I know not how it happened but with the first glimpse of the landscape, a sense of insufferable gloom pervaded my spirit. I looked upon the scene of depression; I couldn't compare it to anything. It was melancholic, it was quiet and poetic, and I was all alone in the midst of unlimited white and grey.
